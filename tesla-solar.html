Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tesla Solar Roof?

+

Tesla Solar Roof is an integrated roofing and solar solution that replaces your conventional roof with interlocking glass tiles containing solar cells. It combines the durability of a premium roof with the energy generation benefits of solar panels, providing a seamless, elegant aesthetic.

How does Tesla Solar Roof differ from traditional roofing?

+

Tesla Solar Roof tiles are engineered glass tiles that are both weatherproof and generate electricity. Unlike traditional roofing combined with separate solar panels, Tesla Solar Roof integrates both functions into one system, reducing complexity and improving the home's curb appeal.

Is my home suitable for Tesla Solar Roof?

+

Most homes in Los Angeles are suitable for Tesla Solar Roof installation. Ideal conditions include good sun exposure, adequate roof space, and roof structure that can support the weight of the system. Our team conducts a free assessment to determine if your home qualifies.

What are the energy savings with Tesla Solar Roof?

+

Energy savings depend on your home's energy consumption, roof size, sun exposure, and local utility rates. Most homeowners see significant reductions in their electricity bills. Our team provides detailed energy production estimates during your consultation.

What warranty and maintenance is required?

+

Tesla Solar Roof comes with comprehensive warranty coverage for the roof tiles and electrical components. Maintenance requirements are minimal, with occasional cleaning recommended for optimal energy production. Our team provides full maintenance guidelines during installation.
