Bel Air Roofing Services

Premium roofing solutions for Bel Air homes

Bel Air represents the pinnacle of Los Angeles residential living, and the roofing on these estates must reflect that distinction. WesTec Roofing specializes in the high-end materials and complex installations that Bel Air properties require, bringing meticulous attention to detail on homes where anything less than exceptional simply will not do.

Architecture & Roofing in Bel Air

Behind the gates of Bel Air, architectural styles range from Mediterranean Revival estates with sweeping clay tile roofscapes to ultra-modern glass-and-steel residences where standing seam metal and concealed flat roof membranes are essential design elements. French chateau-inspired properties along Bellagio Road and Copa de Oro feature steep mansard rooflines clad in natural slate, often with ornamental copper ridgecaps, dormers, and integrated gutter systems. Many newer builds push the boundaries of contemporary design, incorporating green roof sections, rooftop decks over waterproofed membranes, and expansive skylights that require precision flashing details.

Common Roofing Challenges in Bel Air

Bel Air's dramatic hillside topography creates some of the most demanding roofing conditions in Los Angeles. Steep, winding roads and gated properties mean equipment access must be carefully planned, sometimes requiring crane lifts for material delivery to ridgeline homes above Stone Canyon. The community falls within a Very High Fire Hazard Severity Zone, mandating fire-resistant roofing assemblies with Class A ratings on all installations. With roof footprints regularly exceeding 5,000 square feet and some surpassing 10,000, the scale of these projects requires precise project management and a crew experienced in coordinating complex architectural forms with numerous hips, valleys, and penetrations.

Standing Seam Metal

Architectural standing seam metal roofing for Bel Air's contemporary and transitional-style residences. We fabricate custom panels on-site for seamless runs on long roof planes, and offer a range of finishes from zinc-coated steel to natural copper.

Barrel and flat clay tile installation for the Mediterranean and Spanish-inspired villas that are central to Bel Air's architectural identity. Our team handles everything from matching salvaged tiles on restoration projects to full tear-off and replacement with modern underlayment systems.
