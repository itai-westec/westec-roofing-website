Brentwood Roofing Services

Premium roofing solutions for Brentwood homes

Brentwood is one of the Westside's most desirable residential neighborhoods, known for its leafy streets, family-friendly atmosphere, and mix of architectural styles spanning decades. WesTec Roofing has the experience to work across the full range of Brentwood homes, from ranch estates along Tigertail Road to contemporary builds tucked into the upper canyons above Mandeville.

Architecture & Roofing in Brentwood

Lower Brentwood features a classic Southern California residential character, with traditional ranch-style homes and Colonial Revival houses from the 1950s and 60s that typically carry composition shingle or concrete tile roofs. Moving uphill toward Mandeville Canyon and Sullivan Canyon, the architecture shifts to larger custom homes on hillside lots, many featuring mid-century modern lines with flat or low-slope roof systems. Newer luxury construction throughout the neighborhood often blends indoor-outdoor living with a combination of flat roofing sections and standing seam metal accents, requiring a roofer who can handle multiple systems on a single project.

Common Roofing Challenges in Brentwood

Upper Brentwood's proximity to the Santa Monica Mountains places many homes within designated fire hazard severity zones, making Class A fire-rated roofing materials a requirement rather than an option. Hillside lots present drainage challenges where improperly sloped or maintained roofs can direct water toward foundations on steeply graded properties. The neighborhood's mature sycamore and coast live oak canopy, while beautiful, drops significant leaf and branch debris that clogs gutters and accumulates in roof valleys, accelerating wear and creating conditions for moisture retention and algae growth.

Our Brentwood Roofing Services

Shingle Roofing

High-performance composition shingle systems for Brentwood's traditional homes, including impact-resistant and Class A fire-rated options required for properties near the canyons. We help homeowners choose profiles and colors that complement their home's existing character.

Flat Roof Systems

TPO and PVC membrane installations for Brentwood's mid-century modern and contemporary homes where low-slope design is integral to the architecture. Proper drainage engineering is critical on hillside lots, and we design every flat roof system with tapered insulation to eliminate ponding.

Emergency Roofing Services

Rapid response for storm damage, fallen branches, and wind-related roof failures that Brentwood canyon homes are particularly vulnerable to during winter storms. Our crew provides same-day tarping and expedited permanent repairs to minimize interior damage.
