Our Story

For three decades, WesTec Construction has built and restored some of LA’s most distinguished homes — from modern estates in Malibu to classic residences in Bel Air. That depth of experience shaped a simple belief that guides everything we do: take care of the client, and the work takes care of itself.

When we launched WesTec Roofing, we brought that same standard to every shingle, tile, and seam. Whether it’s a full roof replacement, an emergency tarp in a storm, or a Tesla Solar Roof installation, the approach is the same: service first, quality always.
